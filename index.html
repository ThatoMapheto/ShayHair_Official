Lace Types

HD Lace

Our HD lace is the thinnest and most transparent lace available on the market. It blends seamlessly with all skin tones, making it virtually undetectable when applied. This creates the most natural-looking hairline possible.

Ideal for: Clients seeking the most natural appearance, special events, and everyday wear.

Transparent Lace

Our transparent lace offers a great balance between durability and natural appearance. It's slightly more durable than HD lace while still maintaining a natural look that melts into the skin.

Ideal for: Regular wear, clients new to lace wigs, and those who want a natural look with added durability.

Care Instructions

To maintain the quality and longevity of your hair:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Use heat protectant when styling
  • Store on a wig stand when not in use
  • Brush gently starting from ends to roots
  • Wash every 2-3 weeks depending on use
